pasta recipe:


Why buy the boxed stuff when you can make noodles

at home? This homemade pasta recipe is way better than what you can buy at the

store and it's surprisingly easy to throw together

with just five ingredients you likely already have on hand!

Ingrediant:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes
